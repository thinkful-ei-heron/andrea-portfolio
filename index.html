About Me

Aspiring web developer that is currently enrolled in Thinkful’s software engineering immersion course. My drive to turn information into action contributes to my pursuit of academic advancement. I try to keep a mindful and fierce approach to test-driven development through my background in technical training and client relations.


My background in biology has taught me that with any experiment there will be a lot of failure and some successes, but the drive and perseverence through those experiences make the end result feel like a greater accomplishment. From working in a parasitic manipulation lab, the Forest Service, and a top craft ten brewery in the nation I have learned how to adapt and go from knowing little to feeling accomplished and comfortable in each of those different fields. I am ready to bring my passion and drive for success to any workplace I am at.


Born and raised in West Palm Beach, FL, but currently based in Boulder, CO. When I’m not coding, I enjoy climbing in the alpine, reading about divisive issues that shape our society, adventuring through new places, or relaxing with some yoga.
